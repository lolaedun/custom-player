Playing, Cutting and Trimming Audio

To Play, Cut or Trim an audio file, you can specify the start and end points.

For instance, if you want to Play, Cut or Trim the audio from the 10th to the 20th second, simply input "10" as the starting point and "20" as the ending point, and then click the "Play, Cut or Trim" button.
